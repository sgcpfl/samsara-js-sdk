updateDriver

  • updateDriver(requestParameters: UpdateDriverOperationRequest): Promise<DriverResponse>
    • Defined in apis/DriversApi.ts:220

    Update a specific driver's information. This can also be used to activate or de-activate a given driver by setting the driverActivationStatus field. If the driverActivationStatus field is 'deactivated' then the user can also specify the deactivatedAtTime. The deactivatedAtTime cannot be more than 6 months in the past and must not come before the dirver's latest active HOS log. It will be considered an error if deactivatedAtTime is provided with a driverActivationStatus of active. Update a driver
